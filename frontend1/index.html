Our Services

At Sampada Distributors, we are dedicated to providing exceptional services that cater to your needs.

Supplier and Purchase Management

This service supports the management of suppliers and purchase orders. It includes functionalities like maintaining supplier databases, tracking purchase orders and deliveries, managing payment terms, and generating reports on supplier performance and costs.

Reporting and Analytics

The system offers reporting and analytics services to provide insights into pharmacy operations. It includes generating reports on sales, inventory levels, profitability, prescription trends, and other key performance indicators. These reports help in making informed business decisions and identifying areas for improvement.

Inventory Management

This service allows the pharmacy to manage its inventory of medicines and other healthcare products. It includes functionalities such as adding new medicines, updating quantities, tracking expiration dates, generating purchase orders, and managing stock levels.
